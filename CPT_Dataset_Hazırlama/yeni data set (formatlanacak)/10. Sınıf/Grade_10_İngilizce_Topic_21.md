#### **THE PASSIVE**

We form "the passive" with the verb to be in the appropriate tense and the past participle of the main verb.

We use "the passive"

• when the person who carries out the action is unknown, unimportant or obvious from the context.

The arrival of the new year is celebrated.

Football is liked all around the World. Switzerland is known for its chocolate.

#### **SEQUENCE ADVERBS**

Sequence Adverbs are used to

![](_page_0_Picture_26.jpeg)

describe a series of events in relation with another and the order in which they occurred or to describe a procedure. You can start the description using: First, continue with Then, next and conclude with Finally. These adverbs are usually placed at the beginning of a sentence.

#### **OUESTION TYPES**

➤ Questions asking time: When is it celebrated?

When does it take place?

- ➤ Questions asking feelings & opinions: How does it impact people?
  - What are the general impressions?
- ➤ Questions asking events & activities: What are the main events & activities?

What happens during the day / festival?

➤ Questions asking about preparations: How do people prepare for it?

What do the preparations include?

➤ Questions asking for purposes: Why is it special?

Why is it celebrated?

What makes it important?
