#### **Describing Places:**

We use 'There is / There are' to say that something exists.

We use there is before singular nouns and there are before plural nouns.

#### COMPARATIVE FORM OF ADJECTIVES

We use compartive forms of adjectives to compare two people, things, places, etc. We usually use than with

comparative adjectives.

#### THERE IS / THERE ARE

#### **Affirmative:**

| There is / There are | Object(s)     | Place               |
|----------------------|---------------|---------------------|
| There is             | a sofa        | in the living room. |
| There are            | two paintings | on the wall.        |
