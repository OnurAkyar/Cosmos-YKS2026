- **3. Answer the following questions according to the information given in the letter.**

- a) Who is a dentist? Richard's father, John, is a dentist.
- b) How many people are there in Richard's family? There are five people.
- c) What's London like? The weather is changeable in London.
- d) What languages does Amelia speak? She speaks French, Turkish and English.
- e) Which school does Julia attend? She attends primary school.
- **4. You are ill and you need a doctor. So, you ask:**
- a) Where is the nearest pharmacy?
- b) How can I go to the butcher?
- c) Where is the post office?
- d) Could you tell me where the hospital is?
- e) Do you want to see a doctor?
