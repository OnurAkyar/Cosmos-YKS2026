#### Agreeing/Disagreeing

Agreeing: To say we feel the same, we say; Yes, I agree or Yes, you're right.

#### **Examples:**

A: I think the film is too long.

B: Yes, I agree.

A: The music is the best part.

B: Yes, you're right.

Disagreeing: To disagree with someone's opinion, we can say;

I don't agree or I don't think so.

### Example:

A: That's a terrible film!

B: I don't agree. / I don't think so.
