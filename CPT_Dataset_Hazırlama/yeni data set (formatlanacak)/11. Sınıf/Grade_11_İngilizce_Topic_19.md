## **KONU** OPEN YOUR HEART

#### PAST MODALS (Part 2):

#### CAN'T HAVE

We use can't + have + past participle (V3) to say that it is almost certain that this was impossible.

Fiona can't have gone bungee jumping with her fiancé; she has a fear of heights.

Robert can't have been at the meeting; he was out of town.

#### MAY / MIGHT / COULD HAVE

We use may / might / could + have + past participle to say it is likely that something would have happened in the past if circumstances had been different, or to say that by sometime in the future it is likely that something will have happened.

**NOTE:** We don't use can + have + past participle.

I may / might / could have accepted the job

offer if it had been in London.

I could have gone to my aunt for advice but she was abroad. (it was possible but it didn't happen.)

Jennifer may / might have seen a psychologist about her phobias. (Perhaps she did.)

#### New Words:

- a. Dim: not having much light I could see a dim light when I entered the house.
- **b. Injured:** hurt or physically harmed The dog was badly injured after a car had hit it.
- **c. Wreck:** badly damaged vehicle We saw a ship wreck while we were walking by the beach.
- **d. Kitten:** a very young cat He realised that there was a hungry and shivering kitten under his car.
- **e. Deserted:** empty, no people around The police found the stolen car in a deserted area.

### **SORULAR:**

1) Aşağıdaki soruda boş bırakılan yere uygun gelen ifadeyi işaretleyiniz.

Aria was crying when I met her at the dormitory. - - - -.

- A) Something bad might have been happened
- B) She must have won the chess tournament
- C) It should have been an ordinary day
- D) Everything must have been okay
- E) She can't have seen me

Cevap: A

**2)** Aşağıdaki soruda, verilen cümleye anlamca en yakın cümleyi işaretleyiniz.

He might have been less dismissive of my talents if he could have seen my latest achievements.

- A) He was so rude that he didn't even understand my success and skills.
- B) I wanted him to be aware of my success and skills, but he was far away from me.
- C) If I had shown him how much I was skilled, he had to recognize my achievements.
- D) I thought him as a tolerant man, but he was so proud that he even ignored my success.
- E) He hadn't been aware of my recent accomplishments, so he didn't care about my skills.

Cevap: E

**3)** Aşağıdaki soruda boş bırakılan yere uygun gelen ifadeyi işaretleyiniz.

The boy was still trembling. He - - - - have been frightened.

- A) couldn't
- B) shouldn't
- C) should
- D) can't

E) must

Cevap: E
