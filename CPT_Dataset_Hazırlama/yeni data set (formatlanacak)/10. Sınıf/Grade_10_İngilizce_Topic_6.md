## KONU PLANS

### Future with be going to and will

There are different ways in English that you can talk about the future. We generally use "will" and "going to" to speak about the future.

■ We use "be going to" when we decided to do something in the future.

**Examples:** I'm going to drive there and talk to her.

We are going to practise more and defeat them next time.

We are going to go fishing tomorrow morning.

■ We use "will" for what we think will happen in the future.

**Examples:** I think she will come to my birthday party.

We use "will" when we decide to do something at the moment of speaking.

**Examples:** A: What would like to have? Tea or coffee?

B: I will have tea

# SUGGESTIONS When you're m

When you're making suggestions in English, you're putting forward a plan or an idea for another person to think about.

#### **MAKING SUGGESTIONS**

Examples: Why don't we go to a dance festival?

How about going to the cinema? Let's watch a horror film. Shall we invite Sam to the party? Would you like to go out tonight?
