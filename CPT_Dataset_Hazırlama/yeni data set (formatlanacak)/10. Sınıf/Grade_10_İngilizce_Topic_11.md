## Used to

We use 'used to + infinitive'

• For things that happened repeatedly or over a long period of time in the past. These things are not true now.

**e.g.** I **used to go out** a lot when I was a teenager. (Now, I usually stay home.)

I **used to go** to bed early when I was a student. We **didn't use to live** in a village ten years ago. **Did** you **use to go** to school on foot or by school bus?
