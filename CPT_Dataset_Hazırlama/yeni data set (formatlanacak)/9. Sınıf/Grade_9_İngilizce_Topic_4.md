### **KONU** EVİMİZİN BÖLÜMLERİ (PARTS OF OUR HOUSE)

- Evimizin bölümleri, esya isimleri, yer bildiren edatlar
  - Odamızı/evimizi betimlemek. KONU ÖZETİ:

Parts of a house: kitchen (mutfak). bathroom (banyo), bedroom (yatak odası), garage (garaj), living room (oturma odası), study room (çalışma odası), dining room (yemek odası), balcony (balkon)

Objects in a house:

- Kitchen: fridge, cooker, cupboard, dishwasher, sink
- Living room: sofa, carpet, cushions, armchair, coffee table
- Bedroom: wardrobe, bedside table, bed, drawer, mirror
- Bathroom: sink, bath, towel, shower, closet, washbasin
- Study room: desk, chair, bookcase Prepositions of place: in, on, under, between, near, next to, in front of, behind, beside

#### Describing a room in a house:

#### -My bedroom:

I have got a small but cosy room. My bed is near the window. There is a bedside table next to my bed. There is a lamp on the bedside table. The wardrobe is opposite my bed. The bookcase is near the door of the room. And there is my study desk and a chair between the wardrobe and the bookcase. There are some posters on the wall opposite the desk. I love them very much. And, the carpet is in the middle of the room on the floor.
