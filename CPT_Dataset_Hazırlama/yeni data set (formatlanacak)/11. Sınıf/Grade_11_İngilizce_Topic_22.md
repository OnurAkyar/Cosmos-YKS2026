#### **COMPARATIVES/SUPERLATIVES**

We use

• the comparative to compare one person, animal, thing, place, etc with another. We can use than with the comparative.

Celine has a **larger** flat **than** Jill. Our new pilates instructor is **younger** than the previous one.

• the superlative to compare one person, animal, thing, etc, with two or more people, animals, things, etc, in the same group. We use the...of / in with the superlative.

Jason is **the friendliest** boy in the class. Palandöken is **the largest** ski run in Europe. Sheila is **the best** of all windsurfers in the club. It was **the most** exciting flight I've ever had.
