#### **Making Comparisons**

We use the *superlative* form to compare one person, thing, animal or place with several of the same kind (with the whole group).

#### a. one-syllable adjectives:

| Adjective | Superlative  |
|-----------|--------------|
| short     | the shortest |
| small     | the smallest |
| big       | the biggest  |
| hot       | the hottest  |

### b. two-syllable adjectives ending in -y:

| Adjective | Superlative     |
|-----------|-----------------|
| happy     | the happiest    |
| easy      | the easiest     |
| friendly  | the friendliest |

## c. adjectives with two or more syllables:

|           | Adjective | Superlative        |
|-----------|-----------|--------------------|
| beautiful |           | the most beautiful |
| expensive |           | the most expensive |
| famous    |           | the most famous    |

#### d. irregular adjectives

| Adjective | Superlative             |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| good      | the best                |
| bad       | the worst               |
| far       | the furthest / farthest |

# Some expressions used by a shop assistant in shopping:

- May I help you?
- How can I help you?
- · What size are you?
- · What size do you wear?
- · What color do you prefer?
- Would you like to try it/them on?
- The fitting rooms are over there.
- The T-shirt suits you very well.
- That's 70 liras altogether.

# Some expressions used by a customer in shopping:

- I'm looking for a pair of trousers.
- Do you have these jeans in a smaller size?
- Do you have these shoes in size 38?
- · Can I try it/them on?
- They don't fit me. / It doesn't fit me.
- It's too small/ big/ tight/ short/ long.
- I want a smaller size, please.
- · Could I have a receipt, please?
- · I'm an extra large.

#### ÖRNEK SORULAR

Aşağıdaki sorularda uygun seçeneği verilen parçaya göre işaretleyiniz.

İstanbul, Ankara and İzmir are the first three biggest cities in Turkey. My favourite city is Ankara because I like the weather in Ankara. İstanbul is the most populous city but if I want to shop, I prefer İstanbul because it has the biggest shopping malls and stores with low prices. In summer, I prefer İzmir because it has beautiful holiday resorts.

- 1. İstanbul - -.
- A) is hotter in summer
- B) has the best climate
- C) is the best city to shop
- D) is the biggest in the world
- E) has nice holiday opportunities

Cevap: C

- 2. The author thinks Ankara - -.
- A) has a nice climate
- B) is the coldest city in Turkey
- C) has the best holiday options
- D) is the most crowded city of Turkey
- E) is the only place to go in summers

Cevap: A

- 3. İzmir - -.
- A) has stores with low prices
- B) doesn't have any shopping malls
- C) is more crowded than other cities
- D) hosts more people than other cities
- E) is a better city to have a holiday in summer

Cevap: E
